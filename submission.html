Submission Guidelines

Scope

The journal accepts original research articles in pure mathematics, applied mathematics, stochastic processes, optimization, computational mathematics, and related interdisciplinary areas.

Manuscript Preparation

  • Manuscripts must be written in clear academic English.
  • LaTeX format is strongly recommended.
  • Include abstract, keywords, and MSC classification.
  • All references must follow a consistent citation style.

Peer Review Process

All submissions undergo a rigorous peer review process conducted by experts in the relevant field. The review model is double blind unless otherwise specified.

Submission Email

Submit manuscripts to: journal@emaildomain.com
