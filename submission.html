Journal of Mathematical Continuum

Submission Guidelines

1. Types of Contributions

The Journal of Mathematical Continuum welcomes original research articles, substantial review papers, and significant theoretical contributions that advance the mathematical sciences. Submissions must present novel findings, rigorous proofs, or meaningful methodological developments within the scope of pure mathematics, applied mathematics, stochastic analysis, optimization, computational mathematics, and related interdisciplinary quantitative disciplines.

2. Manuscript Preparation

Authors are expected to prepare manuscripts with clarity, precision, and formal mathematical rigor. The journal strongly encourages submission in LaTeX format to ensure high quality typesetting of mathematical expressions.

  • Manuscripts must be written in clear and grammatically correct academic English.
  • The paper should include a concise abstract summarizing key results.
  • Provide relevant keywords and Mathematics Subject Classification (MSC) codes.
  • All theorems, propositions, and proofs must be clearly stated and logically structured.
  • References must be complete, accurate, and formatted consistently.
  • Figures and tables should be properly labeled and cited in the text.

3. Originality and Ethical Standards

Submissions must represent original work that has not been published previously and is not under consideration elsewhere. Authors are responsible for ensuring the authenticity of their research and proper citation of all sources. The journal adheres to strict standards of academic integrity and may employ plagiarism detection tools when necessary.

4. Peer Review Process

All manuscripts undergo a rigorous peer review process conducted by experts in the relevant field. The journal follows a double blind review model to ensure impartiality and scholarly fairness. Editorial decisions are based on originality, mathematical rigor, significance of results, and clarity of exposition.

5. Submission Procedure

Manuscripts should be submitted electronically in PDF format along with the source LaTeX files. A cover letter briefly describing the contribution and its significance is encouraged.

Submit manuscripts to: janardan@ravenshawuniversity.ac.in

6. Copyright and Publication

Upon acceptance, authors may be required to complete a copyright agreement. The journal reserves the right to edit accepted manuscripts for clarity, formatting, and consistency while preserving the integrity of the content.
