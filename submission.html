Journal of Mathematical Continuum

Submission Guidelines

1. Scope of Submissions

The Journal of Mathematical Continuum invites original research articles, comprehensive review papers, and substantial theoretical contributions across the spectrum of mathematical sciences. Submissions should advance mathematical knowledge through rigorous analysis, innovative methodology, or significant theoretical development in pure mathematics, applied mathematics, stochastic processes, optimization theory, computational mathematics, and interdisciplinary quantitative research.

2. Manuscript Structure and Formatting

Authors are expected to prepare manuscripts with clarity, precision, and strict adherence to mathematical rigor. The journal strongly recommends preparation in LaTeX to ensure accurate representation of mathematical expressions and formal structures.

  • Include a concise and informative abstract.
  • Provide relevant keywords and Mathematics Subject Classification codes.
  • Clearly state definitions, theorems, lemmas, and proofs in a logically coherent manner.
  • Ensure all mathematical notation is consistent throughout the manuscript.
  • Provide complete and properly formatted references.
  • Figures and tables must be clearly labeled and referenced within the text.

3. Originality and Academic Integrity

Submitted manuscripts must represent original research that has not been previously published and is not under consideration elsewhere. Authors are responsible for ensuring proper citation of prior work and adherence to ethical research standards. The journal maintains strict policies against plagiarism, data fabrication, and unethical publication practices.

4. Peer Review Policy

All submissions undergo a rigorous peer review process conducted by experts in the relevant domain. The journal follows a double blind review model to ensure fairness, impartiality, and scholarly objectivity. Editorial decisions are based on originality, mathematical depth, clarity of exposition, and contribution to the advancement of the discipline.

5. Submission Procedure

Manuscripts should be submitted electronically in PDF format along with the corresponding LaTeX source files. A brief cover letter describing the significance and originality of the work is encouraged.

Submit manuscripts to:
janardan@ravenshawuniversity.ac.in

6. Publication and Copyright

Upon acceptance, authors may be required to complete a copyright agreement or publication consent form. The journal reserves the right to make minor editorial modifications for clarity, formatting consistency, and typographical accuracy while preserving the scientific content and integrity of the manuscript.

7. Publication Frequency

The journal follows a continuous publication model within each volume. Accepted articles are published promptly upon completion of the review and production process.
