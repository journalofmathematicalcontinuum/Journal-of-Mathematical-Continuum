Welcome

The Journal of Mathematical Continuum is an international peer reviewed scholarly platform dedicated to advancing rigorous research across the full spectrum of mathematical sciences. The journal fosters foundational inquiry, analytical precision, structural innovation, stochastic modelling, optimization methodologies, and computational advancements that collectively shape the evolving continuum of modern mathematics.

Aims and Scope

The journal publishes original research articles in pure mathematics, applied mathematics, probability theory, stochastic processes, optimization, mathematical modelling, computational mathematics, and interdisciplinary quantitative sciences. Emphasis is placed on theoretical depth, methodological rigor, analytical clarity, and meaningful scientific contribution.

Peer Review Policy

All submissions undergo a rigorous peer review process conducted by subject experts. Manuscripts are evaluated for originality, mathematical correctness, clarity of exposition, and scholarly significance. The journal is committed to maintaining the highest standards of academic integrity.

Call for Papers

The Journal of Mathematical Continuum invites high quality original manuscripts that contribute significantly to theoretical and applied mathematical sciences. Authors are encouraged to submit manuscripts prepared in LaTeX format along with appropriate classifications and keywords.
